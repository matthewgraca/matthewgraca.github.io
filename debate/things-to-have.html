Things to have

Most of these things are just nice to have. I would say that the only thing that is strictly required is Verbatim, but everything else is really up to preference.

Materials

  • Table tote
  • Paper (get something stylish like blue paper)
  • Ultra fine point pens wow
  • Swaggiest timers known to man. go to the local daiso on Las Tunas, or be boring and buy vanilla kitchen timers

Software

  • Verbatim: the only truly required item on this list. software that makes debate card cutting and document managing infinitely easy

Online Resources

  • Debate blog run by Scott Phillips, super good coach. great insights about the game
  • Debate wiki. see what the best teams are running after every tourney
  • Files from all the camps. the best resource on this list bar none.
